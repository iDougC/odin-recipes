Lasagna Recipe

Description

A classic 'italian' dish, this recipe is sure to be winner for all the family

Ingredients

  • 1 Can chopped tomato
  • 4 sheet lasagne
  • As much cheese as humanely possible
  • 100g beef/turkey mince
  1. Cook beef mince
  2. Mix with chopped tomatoes
  3. Layer with lasagne sheets
  4. Drown in cheese over the top(and throughout for molten cheesy goodness)
  5. Cook in oven for 30 mins on 200 degrees
